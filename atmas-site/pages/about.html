About Us

ATMAS was born from the vision and determination of three Chinese partners who, with nearly 25 years of experience in trade and logistics, sought to leverage that expertise to explore the maritime sector in Panama. Ship provisioning in the region was underserved, with many unmet needs impacting efficiency, delivery speed, service quality, and return on investment for both carriers and vessels alike

Our Mission

Revolutionizing this sector in the region to improve the lives and livelihoods of associated crews and the commercial prospects of our shipping partners.

ATMAS’s offices and warehouses, strategically located on Avenida Domingo Díaz, San Antonio, Panama City, cover an impressive space of 46,231.28 square meters. This space allows us to store a wide range of dry, perishable, and fresh products, ensuring we always have essential items in stock for the vessels transiting Panamanian waters.

Our Vision

Not just selling products — it’s about:

  • Revolutionizing the maritime sector.
  • Improving the livelihood of crews.
  • Enhancing the commercial success of shipowners.
  • Expanding toward environmentally sustainable practices.

Our Values

  • Commitment: Responsibility in every delivery.
  • Quality: Certified and verified products.
  • Innovation: Continuous improvement in processes and technology.
  • Transparency: Clear and honest communication.
